# VISPAN
Real-time visualization of multiplex syndromic PCR panel sequencing for rapid pathogens detection.

![](docs/images/main.png)


## Motivation
Infectious diseases remain a major public health concern. Their emergence and (re)emergence can be driven by many factors, and rapid detection and characterization of (re)emerging pathogens are therefore critical for effective outbreak management and for improving our understanding of epidemics.
In this context, we present a tool that enables real-time visualization of the results of a multiplex PCR syndromic panel in combination with Oxford Nanopore Technologies sequencing. Our tool enables rapid pathogen detection and provides real-time monitoring of sequencing and diagnostics at the amplicon level.
